Dog Sports Competitor

I compete in multiple dog sports with my dogs. Through these sports, I have met a great community of people and improved my relationship with my dogs. Ultimately, this has inspired me to become a dog trainer.

K9 Toss & Fetch

There are clubs all over the world that compete in this world wide league. The basic idea is to throw a disc as far as you can and have your dog catch it. The further the throw, the more points you get.

Dock Diving

Dock diving is a dog sport in which dogs compete in jumping for distance or height from a dock into a body of water. It is thought to have originated in 1997 in the United States.

K9 Nose Work

K9 Nose Work is a fun search and scenting activity that can be enjoyed by all dogs and people. It is a great way for your dog to have fun, build confidence, and burn lots of mental and physical energy.
